# Goals & Non-Goals

## Goals

- Provide a visual workflow designer for XState machines.
- Run workflows durably on Restate with automatic retries and timers.
- Support multi-tenancy with isolated company namespaces.
- Allow extensible action and guard catalogs per tenant or platform.
- Offer a superadmin interface for platform management.
- Keep local development simple with Docker Compose.

## Non-Goals

- Replacing XState as the workflow DSL.
- Providing a fully generic BPMN-style editor.
- Hosting as a managed SaaS in this codebase.
- Supporting databases other than SurrealDB.
